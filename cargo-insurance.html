Cargo Insurance Services

At EWNS Global Logistics, we provide reliable and cost-effective cargo insurance solutions designed to protect your shipments from unforeseen risks during transportation. Whether your cargo moves by Sea, Air, Rail or Road, our insurance ensures complete financial protection throughout the logistics journey.

Why Cargo Insurance is Essential?

During global transportation, cargo is exposed to various risks such as accidents, rough handling, natural disasters, piracy, fire, moisture, delays, and theft. Cargo insurance acts as a financial shield that safeguards your goods and prevents unexpected losses that can impact your business.

What Our Cargo Insurance Covers

  • Loss or damage during transit
  • Fire, explosion & natural calamities
  • Theft, pilferage & burglary
  • Damage due to rough handling or loading/unloading
  • Container loss during sea transit
  • Transit delays causing cargo deterioration
  • General Average claims

Industries We Support

Our cargo insurance solutions cover FMCG, Pharmaceuticals, Electronics, Automotive, Chemicals, Textiles, Machinery, Retail, Industrial Equipment, and Export-Import Businesses.
