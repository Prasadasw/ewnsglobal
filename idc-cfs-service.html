Comprehensive ICD & CFS Services

EWNS Global Logistics delivers smooth ICD (Inland Container Depot) and CFS (Container Freight Station) operations designed to reduce dwell time, prevent demurrage, and streamline your end-to-end logistics flow.

Core Capabilities

  • Container stuffing / unstuffing
  • Bonded & general storage (including hazardous segregation)
  • Customs documentation & examination support
  • Weighbridge & scanning
  • Rail connectivity & shuttle services to ports

Why Choose Our ICD & CFS?

Our highly trained operations team, modern handling equipment and strong compliance processes ensure reduced container dwell time, minimal errors and a streamlined logistics flow.

Industries Served

Electronics, FMCG, Pharmaceuticals, Agro, Automotive, Machinery, Industrial goods and more.

FAQ

Yes, we work closely with customs teams for smooth processing.
