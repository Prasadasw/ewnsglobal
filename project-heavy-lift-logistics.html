Project & Heavy-Lift Logistics Solutions

EWNS Global Logistics specializes in handling **oversized cargo (ODC), heavy-lift machinery, industrial equipment, and project-based shipments** across India and international locations. Our project logistics team ensures safe, compliant, and cost-efficient movement from origin to destination, backed by detailed route surveys, load engineering, and 24x7 operations monitoring.

Why Choose EWNS for Project Logistics?

  • Dedicated project logistics experts & engineering support
  • Pan-India heavy-lift equipment & trained operators
  • Strong compliance with safety regulations & road permits
  • 24x7 tracking, reporting & project supervision
  • Cost-effective multimodal options (road, barge, sea freight)

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do you handle over-dimensional cargo (ODC)?
Yes, we manage all types of ODC and heavy-lift cargo including machinery, industrial equipment and oversized structures.

Q: Can you provide route surveys and escort vehicles?
Absolutely. Our team conducts detailed surveys, obtains permits and arranges escorting for safe movement.

Q: Do you manage port handling?
Yes, we coordinate port operations, crane handling, customs clearance and vessel arrangements for breakbulk shipments.
